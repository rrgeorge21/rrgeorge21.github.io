Description:
Laid down in 2183, the Yorktown was set to be designed as an update for the Alliance navy originally as the first of her class in a new line of stealth frigates. However six months of engineering complications, financial strain, and political banter the construction of the new line of vessel was halted and set to produce only one ship due to the enormous financial cost. The engineers, only able to design a one off vessel decided to utilize the new financial freedoms to update the entire vessel. Yorktowns construction didn’t begin until early 2184, and was finished in early 2185. The vessel underwent multiple successful shakedowns, stretching the new ships legs to its limits and was assigned to Commander James Fenix in late 2184 where it quickly earned a reputation amongst the Alliance and other citadel races. After the events of Commander Shepard’s trial and the new SR2 specifications were released, the Yorktown was sent to dry dock in early February 2186 to undergo refits using the Normandy’s performance as a baseline. The engineers involved in the original design of Yorktown were present when the final refits took place. The ship now boasting a new and improved prototype drive core could put out power three fold that of the Normandy, expanding the Yorktowns mission parameters drastically. It’s offensive capability dwarfed that of any frigate and was more comparable to a heavy cruiser, it’s total estimated cost dwarfed that of SR1 and SR2 combined, aligning its itself financially more with a destroyer, earning its nickname among a Alliance financial officials as a “pocket destroyer.” The ships construction and design was originally human and was presented to the council species, after weeks of debate the ship was now a multi-species sanctioned warship taking its primary commands from the Alliance first, and the Council second, the politics of this matter are still in heated debate.
