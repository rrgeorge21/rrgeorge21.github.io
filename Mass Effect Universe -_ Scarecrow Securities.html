Scarecrow Securities

"NON POTESTIS LATERE"


Overview
Company Name: Scarecrow Securities, Scarecrows, SSec
Founders: Syndicate Remnants
Founded: 2201
Current CEO: Kamyrin Gyvolis
Headquarters: Scarecrow Headquarters, Mir		Grade: B
Type: Company
Structure: Sole Ownership
Role: Military Intervention and Secuirties
Markets: Mercenary Work: Major/Robotics: Minor
Description:

Scarecrow Military Complex or simply Crow's by most in the military bussiness fields were a small time company that in recent years has had a major boon of growth thanks to the Alliance Civil War. Joining the conflict on the side of the Attican's the Crows have played a major ally well also continuing to grow with a merging of the Lost and Damned remnants from the Bacalou Skulls and later merging with Syndic thanks to clever business moves by the sole owner Kamyrin. With these they have gone from company that once specialized in automated securities systems to a full blown military power in the Terminus outside the Big Three.

History
  • End of 2200 and Beginning of 2201: In a surprised attack, the Big Three made up of Eclipse, Blood pack, and Blue Suns lunch a all out attack upon both Syndicate and Barony Forces. Neither of which are allied, This leads to tier 3 being a war zone, within days established lines appear. Well resistance is strong both forces slowly are depleted on both resources and manpower. Within in weeks niether stands capable of continuing the conflict this leads to the collapse of both factions. The Big three move in to claim what remains splitting the districts between one another. A small group of Syndicate Guardsmen lead by Zangeef Chang, a armorer for the Syndicate, move from there isolated outpost within former Syndicate territory to the Bacalou Skull's Geist district. Hoping to reform with other units in more friendly territory no longer being capable of holding out. The survivors reform only being 37 strong, with a few injured from breaking through enemy lines. Without capable medical attention the count becomes 32 members, week later reports come in that Syndicate is no more. This leads to there count becoming even less as Guardsmen toss aside there armor and leave, in the end only 24 remain under Zangeefs command.
  • February 2201: Zangeef now in command of the small group makes a decision, no longer a part of there former group they re brand themselves mercenaries, taking on the nickname Scarecrows as a reminder of there guardsmen past. Placing there armor in storage crates they decide to hide there origins and look for jobs to keep there group alive. Hiding in a old storage building they take any jobs they find. During one job for a local gang to take out a competing gang they lose 3 members when there caught off guard by the enemy gang. With such a lose 2 more leave worried that this line of work is no good for them. By the end of the month the group is 19 strong, with such numbers and the money they made from the few jobs they scrapped togather they lay lower hoping to evade the attention of the Big Three still fearing one day there hideout would be found along with there past.
  • March 2201: The small group too there surprise meets with a former member of the Syndicate who seemed to have lucky not been present during the war. A former Geist at that known as Sarah Redfield, with her unable to speak they assist her in finding jobs she can do on her own. Now and then she stops by the lend a helping hand but for the most part does not rejoin the small group. One day well a few members were out they were spotted by a former Syndicate Guardsmen turn Blue Suns merc. Identifying them to his commanding officers it leads to a shoot out as a Drunk Ralso and two other members try and fight off the group of Blue Suns. Killing the Former guardsmen but losing his two campanions, he flees back to the hideout. Soon followed by a group of Blue Suns looking for blood it leads to a shoot out as the group attempt to escape. Losing another member who stayed behind to lay a trap the Blue Suns. They find the building in flames hours later, with no one left alive. Moving to a new location they once again set up, Ralso gets in a great deal of trouble over the matter but in the end it is disregarded due to the fact they had all ready lost to many members. With only 16 members left the group continue to stay low and collect jobs just interested in trying to pass away the days. Zangeef takes up odd jobs fixing armor for other local Mercs and Bacalou Skulls members. Later that month word of the Reapers quickly spread, this leads to 3 members leaving intend on going home and protecting what they have left. The group would go quite for the Reaper conflict uninterested in fighting the war and only trying to scrap together a living.
  • April 2201: A Former member who had left the group months earlier to fight the Reapers finds herself stuck in a medical bed. Having both her legs blown off well engaging Reaper forces her injuries are great and doctors suspect she has little time left. intent on living through it she passes her time wandering about her roommate who had been in Coma when she had been brought in, a Alliance officer from what she could figure. She yearned to make a friend in her final hours, maybe pass on the story of her life so at least one person would remember her, maybe even pass on word of her death to her former comrades.
  • May 2201: the young officer that the former Guardsmen had waited for so long to see awaken finally awoke early in the month. After she had finally regained her senses both girls spoke of themselves, the guardsmen finding out that the officer was a rich born girl, who family had fallen on hard times and she had joined the military as she planned what to do in the future. The girl she learned was named Kamyrin Gyvolis, well her injuries had not been bad well the girls spoke for days, they found the Kamyrin was going to be discharged from the Alliance military. The guardsmen spoke to Kamyrin and attempted to advise her on what to do with her future, one the guardsmen would not be around to see. This would be probably were the seed of the Scarecrows would be placed, as the Guardsmen spoke of her days in the Syndicate and the fearsome armor. The Armor would become a great interest to Kamyrin even in a passion that would for a time be a sole objective of the young girl to find. But well it had been a great time and the two grew close together one night late into the month the Guardsmen passed away. This struck Kamyrin and would lead her on a search to find the armor intent on rebuilding it not only to make a good amount of cash but also as a way to remember the fallen Guardsmen.
  • November 2201: The group would still be continuing there small time operations, taking on what ever job to continue on living. A few members would even make mention of hoping to go to Illium to find if any other members of the Syndicate might have survived, Ralso being a prime person that sought this. But Zangeef would simply remind them they barly made enough money to feed the group, let alone travel to another world, no matter how bad it was Omega they had no were left to go. Thats the only reason such a band had stayed togather for the last few months. With the Reaper War over, they simply hoped to stay away from there enemies the Big Three. This would not go as they had hoped, once again there band would be attacked but this time by Eclipse. Intend on wiping out a small mercenary band, it was only a bonus they were former Syndicate members. But this time they had been prepared even having set up a second hideout encase of attack. Fleeing to the Trythorian District, this had all ready acted as a second place for them to act out of when handling missions but they hoped that the Eclipse mercs would simply become disinterested.
  • May 2202: For months they had passed away the time in silence continuing with there regular odd jobs, anything to survive as they saw it. This month after so much time searching since her leaving medical care Kamyrin would find luck with her and for once in the past year the group of former Guardsmen would find luck themselves. Kamyrin would find Ralso in a drunk state talking of the good old days, much similar in fashion to the way they had got in trouble before. Wandering back to the Hideout he would be followed by the Armorer intent on meeting with him, she would be in for a surprise to find Zangeef and there group. At first hostile and scared worried they might once again have to flee, after a hour long discussion both groups would happily make a deal. Zangeef would hand over the armor they had stored from there Syndicate days in return they would get a job working for Kamyrin aboard her vessel the White Rose.
  • July 2202: Only a month after the former Guardsmen turned mercenaries had joined the Crew of the White Rose did they find themselves in a new adventure. Well still working on Project Temple the first product that the group would make, they found themselves in a bind. Well on Omega the younger Gyvolis siblings had gone out shopping and found themselves surrounded by a local gang. Interested in trying to sell the girls on the slave market they were taken. Not only did this send Kamyrin into a rare rage, it also send the new White Rose group into a panic. Zangeef, Ralso and a few other Guardsman taken on the former armor of there Syndicate days, lunched a all out attack on the gang. Hunting for the girls, they finally found them after having left quite a mess in there tracks, well heading back to this ship they also found themselves in a new bind. The Blue Suns did not take a liking to Syndicate armor even making a appearance in there area and attacked the group. Well no one died the group for a few months stayed away from Omega not intending to fight the Blue Suns. After that little miss adventure the Younger Gyvolis siblings grew closer to there crew members Zangeef and Ralso, making the crew almost into one big family. They would start up working doing training with the Gyvolis siblings teaching them to defend themselves. During this trip they would also gain a new member Sarah who would become Kamyrins loyal bodyguard and even a friend.
  • December 2202: This month would prove to be the biggest stride in the White Rose crew becoming a true business, though later the funds would be turned towards building them into a PMC, that would not be decided tell far later. This month would mark the creation of the Phase 1 Temple series armor, a upgraded version of the former Syndicate Armor. Given the Syndicate having been a well known in its days, many mercenaries and even collectors were interested in buying the armor. Later would lead to new variations being created and though due to its having a few issues Kamyrin was not happy about it would not see nearly as much sales as she had hoped. But with new vigor she would work towards completing the next line of the project.
  • March 2203: During a trip to Horizon, the crew of the White Rose would meet new trouble when there attacked by a band of Pirates looking to raid the colony. Well the would be pirates would quickly get wiped out by local colonial forces, Kamyrin and crew would assist the populace with holding off the attack well colonial militias prepared to finish them off. For this reason the crew decided to stay for awhile being hired by a number of companies on horizon to help train there guards. Even selling there phase 1 temple armor at a discount for the numerous business companies in need of guards. This would lead a few of the former guardsmen to try and talk with Kamyrin about building a PMC that specialized at task like this, aka helping local populaces and clients defend themselves though teaching of security guards. Well Kamyrin was some what disinterested at first she would give it thought, well present on the planet she would first the very location that her parents had disappeared from, her families Mechworks. Which had been sold shortly after her coming into possession of the family due to the amount of debt the family had incurred. She would find its current owners having disappeared during the reaper wars, most likely having been a casualty of the war. The colony had taken on the rights to the location and still were unsure how to handle it, this would lead to Kamyrin being sad over the matter and lead to her later return to the Colony to rebuy the old facility.
  • September 2203: This month would be the turning point, were Kamyrin and her crew would grow more well known and finally turn around there state of debt and make way towards truly becoming a company. They would complete the Phase 2 Temple series which would prove a far better product then its form brother the Phase 1. With its completion and there new found wealth, Kamyrin would finally call a meeting to discuss the crews futures. The talk would last days, a few were interested in staying how they were, a few wanted to recreate the Syndicate all together, though they were few and between. Most wanted to create a PMC, having all ready thought on the subject Kamyrin would agree, but intent on making a PMC very different from many that all ready graced the stars. She spoke of idea that seemed to captivate the crew, aim towards a autonomous defense contracts. Make security measures for our clients that would permit them to operate without there group being constantly present defending them. Such ideas of automated turrets, Mobile AA defenses, bunker systems and even Mech series. Using a quick response team that was always on the move to back up clients who were in dire straights and deal with more offensive missions. The group all agreed to this idea, all that they needed to figure was settling on a name, it would be Zangeef who would in a joking manner mention Scarecrow. The very name they had adopted during there days on omega surviving day by day, well it had mixed feelings to even Zangeef it came as a surprise as they all agreed to it. Officially this day the Scarecrows would be on there way towards becoming a proper PMC, intent on making a name for themselves among the stars.
  • October 2203: Returning to Horizon the newly formed company would buy the rights to the Gyvolis Mechworks. Beginning it reconstruction towards producing a newly created project Celestial, there first attempt at a automated defense system. Making it into a factory to also support increased production of there armor, hiring local populace to work the factory they came into more luck then just that. Having heard word of the reborn Temple armor a number of Syndicate Guardsmen who had gone off to do other work flocked to the new formed group. This would permit a growth in operations for the group as they made a name for themselves. They would also work out a contract with the local populace of Horizon to act as there first Clients helping in the development of a local militia to help protect the colony encase of future attacks.
  • December 2203: The Company would do its first testing of products and train new found members on handling there gear. Making use of Phase 2 armor they would produce uniquely for themselves, they would test out gear against new test type armors and Custom Loki models being prepared for Project Celestial. This operation would have mixed results some armors showing more promise and odd programming in the Loki models. This would ultimately lead to Phase 3 Production of the Temple series the following year based of the results gained during the op. Given there testing they would take up a few large scale operations dealing with pirates for clients in the Terminus systems, gaining a bigger Client base and building up more.

Due to interest in its contract it would be taken off the open market to await dealings with larger client that could lead to a full blown growth of Scarecrow from a small group to a true PMC.
  • October 2204: Finally having gained a foothold with a important client, Scarecrow along with there leader Kamyrin would go and meet with the Amun Sovereignty intent on making a very important deal that could lunch them onto the PMC stage. This would be done with the sale of the Phase 3 contract leading to its productions going straight to the Amun military as there main stead armor. This would also permit the Scarecrows to show off there training and ability in the hope of gaining a PMC contract also out of there deal. These deals would be between the Amun's General Crafin Kod'garal and Kamyrin, as the groups set up a testing area to show of the promise of the armor, along with the Scarecrows abilities and even there newest product Project Celestial. The General showed great favor towards the armor and its capabilities, even going so far as to show a keen interest in the custom Loki mechs which had been built for the scenario. This lead to Scarecrows first major contract, well not military oriented and instead simply supplying a colonial government with armor it still scored a number of smaller contracts with others who now saw Scarecrow as a PMC player.
  • December 2204: Now with a contract that could give them steady funds Scarecrow set off on producing a second facility on Horizon to act as a training center, in the future to train militia's but for now it was aimed towards training new members and former syndicate guardsmen. A new military doctrine needed to be enforced, given the company had been lax with its own training and disciple, they quickly began to recruit more people to join there ranks and train them from the ground up to be specialized in specific combat styles. This led to the creation of the Crows and numerous sub jobs that aimed towards a individual being specialized for certain situations such as guarding POI's, private security, pirate hunting, and colonial defenses. With the Crows being the military arm of the PMC it also began to recruit engineers and developers to assist with the development of new security measures that would lead it to gain new contracts, during this time Scarecrow would become a official PMC as of prior only being a band of mercenaries and engineers. They sharpened themselves into capable of taking on necessary jobs, though never taking on jobs that felt criminal in activity. This would lead to the Scarecrows gaining a White Knight like appearance to employers, which lead to some not being interested in them and others fully supporting such a security company.
  • February 2205: Having spent the last 4 months developing a new structure and training routine for its newly grown numbers, its Crows had become a more disciplined force and the company side had become stable. Finally opening up the training grounds of the new horizon facility to the public it took its first batch of trainees from local horizon militia raising them up to have a much more capable unit protecting Horizon, beyond the local Scarecrows stationed there. It had also gained more former syndicate members and still making use of Temple Phase two armor, they began to plan for the construction of a new series that could separate the PMC from the Amun military that now made common use of the Phase 3 armor. This would not be implemented for a number of months but would begin planning during this time.

Later that month also sending a unit of Crows to support guarding important military points that lie within Reformer hands, but aside from this see little too no combat in the civil war. Well that Civil war continues the Scarecrows look towards the Alliance civil war, not knowing which side to support they find a many civilians entering their Saga complex to train under the pretense of mercenary work, many of which support the Attican insurrectionist side. Figuring that the war had not fully gone all out they stay on the side lines to see which side to join with, but continue to train insurrectionist who come to them, stating they are simply training them in self protection though they do get a good amount of heat from the Alliance military over the training.
  • July 2205: Scarecrows lunch a operation against Pirate forces controlling a former GI shipyard within the Terminus systems Sowilo System, intent on taking over the facility to begin production of their own anti-piracy forces. It becomes heated week long battle as Crows fight through corridors of entrenched pirates that used the facility to supply their own forces, though with supplies running out on the cornered Pirates they begin surrendering and later the facility is captured. Using this as the beginning several other operations are led against former and lost GI facilities that had been forgotten during the Reaper War hoping to expand their facilities in the Terminus systems and expand their own power, though not all operations are a success its seen as enough to make the Scarecrows a force within the Terminus Sowilo System having pushed out major pirate forces present.

Much more powerful than they had been years prior they begin to send advisers to the insurrectionist encampment to help train their new growing military. Knowing this will cause issues with the Alliance, Scarecrows pull out of Horizon and sell of their facilities moving to new facilities in Sowilo system distancing themselves from Alliance retaliation for their support of the rebels. With their support the Scarecrows are able to grow further as Terminus interest that seek to see a free Attican space and create a border between Citadel and Terminus space flock to the Scarecrow ranks.

The Scarecrows begin their deployment throwing their full support behind the Attican state they stop assisting the Reformist of the Batarians, fearing that they might support the Alliance. Well the battle took resources away from the civil war the crows still took part and handled clean up operations on the planet to further establish good relations with Syndic.

But neverless Kam felt certain when the Crows finally made their move both sides would be caught off guard by what they brought to conflict, a fact she very much enjoyed and well the Crows still had troops stationed in Akuze training Attican troops when compared to what they had building up it was but a small fraction of their troop strength.
  • December 2206: with the fleet nearly prepared and word spreading of Akuze soon being targeted the Crows knew they were on the last stretch and continued their build up but given their condition they knew it would not be finished before Akuze got invaded and only hoped that Akuze could hold out for what would come. Out of all the ships they had acquired only a fraction was back up to its state of the art condition and prepared for war. The troops were continuing their training and more were still coming, with the Terminus becoming more keen on seeing a Attican victory if only to ensure their own protection. The next year would show the Crows efforts in full blossom and they would see how far all their effort would get them in the coming months.

Scarecrow Military Assets

Ground Assets



Scarecrows common military forces are classified as 'Crows' a common term for them, well there numbers might be smaller then most other PMC's they favor elite small strike teams then large numbers. Using advance technology and squad based tactics to take down large opposing forces the Crows are specialized in Digging in a defending clients property and the client themselves. Mirrored by the fact they use heavy armor, they favor body guard and security over wet work operations or assaults. The majority of the Scarecrows offensive and defensive strength come from the Crows which are a majority of the personnel within the PMC, many of the members leading into the Civil war are Syndicate Veterans, Former Bacalou Skulls and military Veterans of the Reaper War, ensuring the Crows always had well trained personnel on hand. Thanks to having so many members from divserse military backgrounds ensures the Scarecrows military training program is top class using training from System Alliance, Syndicate, Confederacy and Bacalou Skulls to make their troops a dangerous but military force in the Terminus.

Above the Crow's lies their elite brothers and sisters that also perform duties of officers within the Crow's command structure. Created through a mesh of training from DOST, Geist, Dust Marked and N7 Operatives the Ravens as their known as a extremely well trained and dangerous elite force. Rivaling many other nations special forces they are considered some of the best body guards a clients could acquire from dealing with the Scarecrow's. Given top of the line gear that is even better than the Crows bellow them only ensures they are a deadly fighting forces, along with training as officers every single one of them is capable of assuming control over a Crow unit and ensuring its used to its best abilities.

Well the Scarecrows norm is defense, they have developed offensive means to ensure they can expand their business, developing the Corpus unit to act as their sword and wet work operatives. Many of which were trained by Tyche, Nich or other former Assassins, some of the Corpus themselves were even former wet work operatives or Assassins. Trained to work in small teams of 4 men, they can be tactically inserted in hostile locations and cripple enemy structures or targets, well not as elite as the Ravens they are seen as a more dangerous element then their Crow brethren.

Will'o'Wisps are the least known group of the Scarecrows, rivaling the Corpus unit in size even possibly larger, the Wisps started out as the Scarecrows Omega branch. Given a new name they were meant to watch over Scarecrow interests on Omega without alerting the Big three to the Crows presence. Having grown considerably they have become the Crows intelligence service with many of their members rivaling Corpus, and Crow agents in combat ability but much more secretive about their activities than their crow brothers. They also feature a large number of Raven skilled personnel making them a well tuned intelligence agency capable of black op missions without requiring additional support from Ravens or Corpus. Though often enough the Wisps are placed in command of all Special forces operations, assembling the correct Wisp, Raven and Corpus mix to get the job done when it is necessary.


From Left to Right, Officer Crow Standard Uniform, Crow Standard Uniform, Support Personnel Standard Uniform

Officer Crow Wearing this uniform marks the individual as a officer within the Crow's unit, leading normally a squad of standard crows. The uniform is what many Crow officers wear outside of combat, capable of being used as light armor it can take a few number of blows before completely being useless. Being adopted by the Scarecrows in 2205 as a upgrade edition of Temple series armor meant for Scarecrows expressed use it has since been updated to the Odin Series armor. This has lead to a increase amount of protection, though its seen as unfit for active use in combat zones. When outside of battle it is seen as the required uniform of the day this is to separate crows on guard duty from off duty crows, and in the off chance of them coming under attack the Crow can participate in battle or at least survive tell they acquire their proper armor.

Crows Wearing their standard unfirom is seen as the norm within a Scarecrow base or facility, well it is seen as light armor it was made to be as comfortable as possible. This led to a decrease in its defense but was seen as a worth while sacrifice to ensure the crows wore it when outside of their direct combat duties. The mask of the armor also acts as a limited time rebreather permitting the crows to operate in toxic situations or limited time in space in the off chance their location is attacked by chemical weapons or they are thrown into the dark of space. Its also the common armor used in martial training due to the fact its much more flexible then most other armor's fielded by the Scarecrows. Also Crows are not required to wear their Masks when off duty but are required to have them on their body in some way in the off chance they come under attack, though if the location is seen as hazardous or dangerous they can sometimes be required to wear it at all times.

Support Personnel seen as the non combatants of the PMC, they can be anything from admin to technicians working on vehicles, required to wear their standard uniform when in a dangerous location or well aboard vessels. This is to ensure that even a non combatant can survive a fightfire in the off chance a Scarecrow facility is attacked, featuring less armor then the Crows standard uniform.
