    [---- Biography ----]

    ~ From Riches To Robes ~


    Gabriel was raised an obedient child by an exceptionally wealthy and powerful family of Theed. Coming from such nobility gave Gabriel every opportunity in life from education, relationships to personal interests such as fencing, philosophy, literature and music. It was in one of his lightfoil training sessions where a grey Jedi known as Desra Lodet spotted him. She challenger him to a duel and after defeating him in a bout offered to teach him the ways of the Force. Leaving his proud family behind Gabriel was exposed to a new world full of wonder and wisdom with the Force, but in the same vein, a world that was fighting the growing corruption of the darkside. The young noble was a humble student who listened carefully and trained religiously. His master shown him each corner of the galaxy. She taught him every ability in the Force she knew. They had jumped exploding volcanoes together. They had drowned in oceans together. Been shot out of space numerous times. Been captured, been tortured, escaped prisons, freed slaves and celebrated. Truly they had done everything together and yet the galaxy still threw more challenges at the pair. For Gabriel his final challenge with his Master was a difficult one to bear.


    ~ The End Of His Master ~


    Despite their adventures Gabriel noticed his Master begin to become colder and more isolated. No more could he read her thoughts or understand all her action. He began to see her deceiving others but for what reasons he could not tell. Whilst on a mission in Millinar all became clear when his Master revealed she had turned to the darkside. They were both to join the Sith and bring an end to the Jedi and the thousands of years of conflict. Unconvinced, young Padawan clashed blades with his Master refusing to follow her. Matched in abilities, matched in the Force the two turned to fighting with just their weapons much like that had done on Naboo years ago. After managing to slice though his Master’s lightsaber he continued to attack with both blades until she was eventually fully disarmed. Unable to kill her, Gabriel almost cost himself his own life as blasted him with lightening. Realising he had no choice Gabriel broke her ability and finished her sending her into the dark depths of Millinar.


    ~ The Fall & The Rise ~


    For less than a year Gabriel served the Jedi alone only to find himself flanked by a Sith Lord who he has yet to identify. Despite the Jedi’s training and combat experience, he was simply outclassed in strength, speed and knowledge of the Force. The Sith began to toy with Gabriel offering him to spare his life should he pledge his allegiance to him and the Sith. After refusing adamantly for the third time the Sith decided to take the boys life. Before the Sith Lord could land the fatal blow, Gabriel monetarily stunned the Sith with an electric judgement of his own and made his escape which left him stranded on a far away planet known as Felcuia.

    For years Gabriel remained on Felucia living off what food he could find be it the tropical vegetation or meat from the disturbed creatures that sought to feast off him. To those that did not know Felucia it was best described as a planet that lived. Its known as a dark creature which seeks to tormet and kill its prey, hunting in the day and continuing in the night. Eventually Gabriel came across a small tribe of Ithorian’s who accepted him after he fended off a Rancor known as Galgazlion that used to torment them and creep into their small village and steal their young. After some time training and many attempts, Gabriel slayed the Rancor. Despite his bond with the Ithorian’s when a lost smuggler crashed their ship into the planet Gabriel chose to leave Felucia and seek the Jedi. He was now a far different individual than the one that had arrived. His purpose to save the Jedi was restored.

    [---- Force Abilities----]

    Open current Force abilities 

    Force Sense: This ability is one that is constantly in use, connecting him to the Force and allowing him to bend its power. Also allows him to sense emotions, other force users, and non-force users.

    Precognition (Danger Sense): This ability helps him see glimpses of the future through Force Visions, or even help him predict opponent movements, although the latter being rare however accurate due to his training as a warrior.

    Shatterpoint: Capable of seeing the 'faults' in objects, people, and events this power had a few uses. When applied to an object he was able to see precisely how it came together and formed, allowing him to see the specific weak points in an object and possibly destroy usually indestructible objects with ease. When focused on a person, he could see how that person could affect an event or begin something, as well as allowing him to see something such as former wounds having recently being stitched together. He would be able to see where the stitches form specifically, and then act on it. Finally, when used during an event such as a war, he could see key points in which may turn a battle or event dramatically differently than what was likely. He would be able to apply this to different events, and manipulate them as he sees fit.

    Crucitorn: A simple ability allowing Gabriel to transcend pain beyond what is considered normal for force-users and non force-users.

    Breath Control: Capable of ‘numbing’ his body’s instincts in order to shut itself down after a period of time without oxygen, allowing him to go without breathing for hours, or days by reserving the oxygen already present in his lungs. Alternatively, he can use this power to create a sort of ‘bubble’ to keep out poisonous gas.

    Force Concealment: The ability to ‘conceal’ his Force Signature in multiple ways. He can appear neutral, or appear untrained, or even completely outside the Force.

    Telepathy: If he is able to sense the presence of someone, he can mentally speak to him or her through he Force. For those he has developed a bond with, he can speak to them over what is supposedly an infinite distance. Though those who he knows somewhat, or not at all has to be fairly close to him.

    Force Persuasion: A simple ability that allows him to influence the decision of weak-minded individuals, he has to be able to sense them and has to be fairly nearby for it to be effective. Decisions they would normally never do are easier to resist.

    Psychometry: Allows him to pick up traces of information about the object she is touching, and events surrounding it. Enabling to view these events as if he was there, including the sights, sounds, feelings (physical and emotional) that the wielder of the object experienced. Easier to use on personal objects used frequently, rather than something used once or twice or used by multiple people.

    Force Cloak: Gives him the ability to bend light and sound waves around himself, which allows him to become invisible to droids, Yuuzhan Vong, and people. Requires a great deal of concentration, and consumes much more Force Energy than normal techniques.

    Force Heal: A meditative ability that allows him to heal the wounds, illnesses, and interior damage of not only himself, but others as well.

    Force Valor: This ability increases the resolve; accuracy, speed, and physical power of his allies as well his self.

    Battle Meditation: An ability that allows him to install a greater morale in battle, while reducing the opposition’s will to fight. Most effective when used in large scale battles between two armies, it can also be used during a skirmish, or duel.

    Force Barrier: An offensive, and defensive ability allowing him to create a wall or barrier around a specific target that can absorb physical, energy-based attacks, or even sound. (Twice per battle)

    Force Protection: An ability that allows him to block physical force abilities used against him.

    Force Absorb: An ability that allows him to ‘absorb’ energy-based attacks such as Force Push and Lightning, and redirect it to his own Force Energy. (Twice per battle)

    Alter Damage: An ability Gabriel uses to reduce the damage his lightsabers deal, so that instead of killing the opponent he can simply incapacitate them. This power can only be used on lightsabers, and only when he is wielding them.

    Force Weapon: Similar to Alter Damage in a way but just the opposite, this ability allows Gabriel to take a random object and apply the Force in a way to make it resistant to lightsabers, capable of absorbing lightning, and deal more damage than it would normally.

    Force Speed: A simple technique that allows him to dramatically increase his physical speed.

    Force Jump: Another simple ability that enables him to jump higher than the average being.

    Telekinesis: A series of abilities, which basically allow him to move objects forwards, backwards, sideways, and so forth. (Force Pull, Push, Grip, etc.)

    Force Wave: Similar to that of telekinesis, he can release a circular invisible force pushing everything away from her at a flat surface.

    Ballistakinesis: Capable of accelerating objects such as coins to near lethal velocity, and throwing them out.

    Morichro: This ability suspends biological functions (breathing and heart rate) causing the target to fade into a sort of sleep, and make it so they could survive days without food, water, or air for periods of over a year. This technique could also be turned on someone else.

    Electric Judgment: Similar to the common Sith Lightning, the difference being the bolts are gold and cannot kill as easily. Instead the ability would stun an opponent and give them minor burns, along with end up being a tiring ability.

    Animal Friendship: Gabriel has the ability to tame animals through use of the Force. The larger and more savage the beast, the harder to do so.
