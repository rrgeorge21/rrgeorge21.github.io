Alright, here’s an approximate build, mind I’m not into min-maxing, just playing and rp-ing on Core:
Race: Half-Elf
STR 12
DEX 18 (+4)
CON 13 (+1)
INT 14 (for consistent 4 skill points)
WIS 10
CHA 14 (mostly focusing on exploits not requiring high Charisma, the amount of exploits will be upped by equipment)

Skills focus: Persuasion, Perception, Knowledge (Arcana), Mobility

Background: Aldori Swordsman, Skill Focus: Persuasion (for RP, Trickery is covered by other companions)
1: Weapon Finesse (DEX on attack rolls), Wooden Flesh
2: Two-Weapon Fighting
3: Weapon Focus: Dueling Sword
4: Dimensional Slide
5: Accomplished Sneak Attacker (alternative: take Improved Initiative here)
6: Improved Two-Weapon Fighting
7: Improved Initiative, Energy Shield (alternative: take Outflank here)
8: Improved Critical (Dueling Sword)
9: Outflank (alternative: take Accomplished Sneak Attacker here)
10: Lightning Lance
11: Greater Two-Weapon Fighting
12: Slippery Mind (DEX for saves against mind-affecting conditions - I found it very useful on my previous runs, can pass high will checks with Shamira in act 4, for example)
13: Dazzling Display, Dancing Electricity
14: Opportunist
15: Shatter Defenses
16: Spell Resistance
17: Critical Focus
18: Staggering Critical
19: Blind Fight, Greater Spell Resistance (or spam spells to dispel concealed enemies (very annoying later on) and take Hammer the Gap?)
20: Improved Blind Fight

Mythic levels:
1: Defensive Study
2: Weapon Finesse (Mythic) (DEX on damage rolls)
3: Ever Ready
4: Two-Weapon Fighting (Mythic) (malus reduced by 2)
5: Leading Strike
6: Improved Initiative (Mythic)
7: Unrelenting Assault
8: Mythic Sneak Attacker (or arguably better Improved Critical (Mythic))
9: Inspirational Leader
10: Flawless Attacks

I agree. that looks really good, but I would also pass on Accomplished Sneak Attacker or at least delay it and prioritize taking Outflank and Dazzling → Shatter earlier. Those are just stronger.

But at mythic rank 8 you should already have so much additional damage, that one sneak dice really does not make that much of a difference. I think Improved Critical Mythic is better, especially as you already have staggering critical as a feat and it helps you triggering that. Taking an additional mythic ability for thundering blows, rupture restraints or mythic charge might also be an option. There might be others, I am not thinking of right out of the bat. But personally I would probably just take Improved Critical (Mythic).
