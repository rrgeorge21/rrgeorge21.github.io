BX3 Black-Wing MK II Starfighter




Ship Class: Starfighter
Ship Role: Multi-Role Starfighter
Ship Length: 12.48 meters
Agility (A): 4
Defensive (D): 3
Offensive (O): 5
Speed (S): 5
Special Gear: 3 (Astromech Slot, Advanced Targeting Systems, Advanced Stealth System)

Weapons:
  • 4 Advanced Laser Cannons
  • 2 Quick Change Warhead Launchers (4 missiles or torpedoes each)

Maximum Acceleration: 5,000 G
Maximum Atmospheric Speed: 1,500 KM/H
MGLT: 140
Hyperdrive Rating: Class 1.0
Back-up Hyperdrive: Class 12
Crew: 1 Pilot, 1 Astromech

Description:

Built at the Dromund Kalakar Shipyards and inspired by the BX1 Black-Wing Starfighter, the MK II of that line was crafted with various tweaks and heavy overall improvements over the first design. While still favoring a multi-purpose starfighter and attempting to match some of Incom's more successful X-Wing designs, the Black-Wing MK II boasts four laser cannons that offered single, dual or quad firing modes. The proton torpedo launcher remained, though it was upgraded to a dual launcher with quick-change magazine options, allowing the MK II the ability to utilize a multitude of secondary ordinance. While still utilizing an astromech slot and proper shielding, other upgrades and refinements included the installation of an advanced targeting system. As an advanced starfighter, the MK II incorporated the latest military innovations, including experimental stealth gear meant to foil sensors and tracking. With all these latest advancements, the fighter had increased speed, range, and more firepower than a standard X-Wing combined with an increased focus on speed and handling, especially to match Force-sensitve pilots.
