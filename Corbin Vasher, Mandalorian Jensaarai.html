Mandolorian Jensaarai
NAME: Corbin Vasher
FACTION: Mandalorian Enclave
SPECIES: Jalkare (half Human, quarter Nagai, and quarter Miralukan)
AGE: 18

SEX: Male
HEIGHT: 6'2" (188cm)
WEIGHT: 170Lbs (77.11kg)
EYES: Deep Violet
HAIR: Raven Black
SKIN: Fair with a red tint
VOICE: Josh Hutcherson
ALIGNMENT: Neutral Good (Chaotic Good when exposed to Netherworld Energy)

FORCE SENSITIVE: Extremely (even has Force Sight from Miralukan ancestry)
Notable Specialized Force Powers:
Natural Force SightForce FlightBallistakineticsTelekinesisInspire
Mechu-DeruPyrokinesisForce CloakForce Lightning
Force WhirlwindForce StealthBattlemindThought Shield
CombustionForce IllusionForce ValorInstinctive Astrogation
Corbin is particularly gifted in concealing his Force-presence, which makes him appear as non-sensitive. He has mastered this and used it to the point where he does it unconsciously. He did this because his Force Aura is strong that even Force-sensatives with weak Force Sense could still feel his presence, which could be distracting or unnerving for some. He can also hide his presence completely, like he isn't even there, or even make himself to be a void in the Force.
Due to being born on Dyspeth, he has a natural affinity for Illusions and can use Force Cloak to bend light and sound himself to be both invisible and soundless, even to electronic surveillance or Force-dead creatures.
His ability in Mechu-Deru allows him to control his armor using the Force. He can don/doff the armor quickly and can even control it like a droid when outside the armor.
Mechu-Deru combined with piloting skills and Instinctive Astrogation allows him to pilot his ship by himself, even without actually being in the cockpit. He can also perform expert microjumps without a gravity well.
His Pyrokinesis and Force Lightning produce violet flames and lightning.
He was born with a natural Force Sight that can see everything that can interact with the force, with Force-sensitive beings, objects, and places having strong Auras. This allows him to see in total darkness or while blind and allows him to see through Illusions. His Sight is fuzzy in a Force suppression field and doesn't work at all in a Force-dead zone (like any Force power).

STRENGTHS: Physically strong and dexterous. Intelligent and skilled in mechanics, computers, and piloting. Extremely strong in concealing his presence (see above in Force Powers) and Illusions. Force connection and strength is boosted with positive emotions.

WEAKNESSES: Dislikes Hutts and dishonorable people. Won't take insults or threats towards his family, clan, or comrades lightly. More likely to rely on physical prowess in combat than using Force abilities. Extremely allergic to Tabac. Negative emotion weakens his connection to the Force, even possibly to the point of cutting off his connection.

APPEARANCE: (Underneath Armor) Medium length, raven black hair. Dark violet eyes, slightly squinty, rather intense look. Lean, muscular build. Usually grinning, even though a helmet covers his face. Strikingly good looks. Detached earlobes. No widow's peak. Noticeably long eyelashes.

BIOGRAPHY: Trained in the ways of the Mandalorian by his father Zael Vasher (half-Nagai), and simultaneously raised in the Jensaarai Force tradition, and also learned Force techniques from his mother Nyx, who was a half human/half miraluken Dathomiri Witch. Corbin was born on Dyspeth and raised on Susevfi, AKA Suarbi 7/5. He was trained as a Jensaarai by Defender Hakilah Mirolai.
Visited birthplace on Dyspeth and trained with the Disciples of Twilight.

SHIP: Modified Stathos-class freighter called Tra'tena.


EQUIPMENT:

  • Burbr'Katya Mk II ​
  • Burbr'sacitr
  • Burbr'sacitayr
  • Nuhaatyc'bedtr
  • Gaan'sacitadr
  • Burbr

LANGUAGES: Mando'a, Sith, Basic, Binary (understands but can't speak it), Bocce, Paecian, High Galactic, Sy Bysti, Anzati, Rodian, Caamasi

PERSONALITY: Firm follower of the Resol'nare. Enjoys good humor. Respects people who stand for their beliefs. Believes (as a Jensaarai) that the Dark Side of the Force isn't inherently evil, it all depends on how you use it. You have to control your emotions and not let them control you, but neither is it good to suppress or ignore emotions. The Dark Side can be used for good and the Light Side for evil. True balance is knowing how to properly use either for good.

NOTES: He doesn't know it, but he actually uses a third side of the Force other than Light or Dark. Instead of reading off negative emotions like the Dark side, it is powered by positive emotions. (In essence, this is the same as naturally possessing the Many Shades of the Force ability.

DREAMS: Truly uniting the Mandalorian people and culture once more. Learning more about the True Nature of the Force and why he seems to be different from other Force-users.
