    SKILLS

    __Over the years since he first became a sith, Drakus has improved much more than just his own powers in the force. Drakus has through many instances become quite skilled with Electronics, from upgrading and maintaining the electrical systems to his armor and lightsabers. To repairing old holo droids for use in the training room of his own personal Ships training room. All thanks to his ability to grasp new skills, quickly and applying them to improve them. This makes him a fast learner, all he has to do is grasp the basic concept to apply it in training and in field use. Another thing to note about Darth Vyrassu is that over the years he’s become highly skilled in surviving in conditions where most people would die. All thanks to the Training he’d received in wilderness survival, and what he about Urban Survival as a child.

    __Darth Vyrassu is also a highly skilled mechanic and Pilot, having learned to take care of his AI-1 stealth Transport and pilot it when one was not assigned to do so during the war with the Galactic Alliance. And just because he is now Warmaster and has to full might of the Imperium’s military forces at his command does not squander the fact that he still continues to be a pilot when he needs to be. Also, as a warrior. He has a keen tactical mind, and always thinks before he acts. Like a Chess game, he can carefully position himself and push an encounter in the direction he wants most of the time. And if someone can match him step for step, he’s not afraid to respect that person as a worthy adversary.

    LIGHTSABER FORM(S)

    Form V: Shien / Djem So: An Offensive saber form that uses wide two handed blows to batter Enemies defenses and break through. Focusing on Dominating the other. Paired with it's Kinetic wide attacks, and parries followed by a fluid counter attack makes a great style for any Sith Warrior to learn after mastering that of Form I. The only two Draw backs, are the lack of Mobility and requires a lot of energy to keep up the assault.~Skill Level: Mastered~ Form VI Niman: Known for it's balance of all elements of lightsaber combat, combining the techniques from Forms that came before into a less intensely demanding combat style. In practice, Form VI was a combination of older forms (Forms I, III, IV, and V), and all of them in moderation. In the blending, much of the individuality was lost, but the strengths were spread evenly, and there was little weakness in it. Due to its "jack-of-all-trades" nature, the success of this form was largely dependent on the practitioner's intuition, improvisation, and creativity in combat rather than the rote responses derived from other forms. ~Skill Level: Mastered

    INTEGRATED PRINCIPLES

    Tràkata: A form of lightsaber combat developed for taking advantage of a lightsaber blade's ability to be turned on and off, a unique quality in a melee weapon. Quickly shutting off then re-igniting the blade could cause confusion in one's opponents, allowing for diversionary feints in combat. Mixed with Jar'Kai, Tràkata is extremely effective, even against multiple opponents.

    Dun Möch:Dun Möch was a traditional Sith tactic based around psychological warfare. Primarily, the practitioner relied on taunts that exposed an opponents inner doubts and weaknesses, disrupting their concentration. Proper execution of Dun Möch called for great patience and control, a rare trait among the Sith, especially those of the earlier eras.


    STRENGTHS

    __It’s hard to pull out just a few strengths and weaknesses besides the obvious. One of his nonphysical strengths is his outlook on things. No matter how bleak things look he always seems to find the light in the dark. Trying to keep the mood light, and keep things calm. Another is his determination and focus, when it comes to learning new things, as well as his comprehension after seeing something done a few times ties into his determination.

    WEAKNESSES

    __Now his weakness’s he tries to hide as much as possible, these are his temper. But now has developed into a strenght of sorts. Even though he is no longer Jedi he still has to be careful not to let his emoutions rule him. Often flirting when he get a chance to get away with it, another is that if he doesn’t succeed in something he beats himself up about it. Often vocalizing these personal bashing sessions during his training, causing him to push himself a little too far beyond his physical limits.

    PERSONALITY

    __Much like he was as a Jedi Initiate, he's still a smart ass. Still a generally nice guy, but his demeanor has shifted. Still a natural flirt, he no longer hides from his temper. He embraces it to give him strength, seeking to aid the Imperium unite the galaxy. With this goal under his belt, it still didn't change Drakus. Even as Warmaster outside of his duties, he remained a nice guy with a 'go with the flow' Attitude.

    While on the flip side, when he attending to the duties as Warmaster. Drakus undergoes a slight change in personality. Like a switch gets flipped and he becomes much more cold and calculated, much so when actually in combat. Though he still retains some of her 'nice guy' attitude, in the sense that he will not sacrifice troops because he holds each soldiers life in his care seriously and will not throw them to the slaughter, instead he will think outside the box and form a plan to minimize casualties and maximize the effectiveness of his armies.

    BIOGRAPHY

    Recording One: My name is Severice Ty’Laun, I’m a new Initiate with the new Jedi order. This holo-recording is to tell my story, but this first part is hard to tell. Not because it was painful, but because there is little I remember about my parents. I can barely remember their faces, but their names escape me for some reason. I grew up poor having to fight for food, and shelter. My parents died when I was five, staying where ever someone would let me for a night. Often trading favors for shelter, such as assisting with their jobs. I learned to take care of myself early in life. Now, my parents died when I was five and stayed with my aunt till I was 7. She was a horrible lady, I ended up running away. I found myself lost in the lower sections of Coruscant, were the poor lived and clawed for their food.

    Now that you know that, I also had to learn to defend myself. Got into numerous fights and have the scars to show it. Never got a proper education, so most of my knowledge is from what I learned growing up. I’m still young but the order has given me a new path, a hard path but one that I can be proud of. But the whole dark side and light side subject is still hard to take in. To think that all the conflict between the Jedi and sith in the past was due to this slight difference in the view of the force. But I kind of understand it. Well this is the first insight into my early childhood. The next will be how I came to be with the Jedi.]

    Recording Two: This is the second recording of this Holojournal, todays subject is how I came to be with the New jedi order. It was during an adventure up to the upper levels of Coruscant. It was obvious that I wasn’t from that level and some of the teenagers started at me. I don’t know how long I stood there fists clenched as they mocked me and called me names before my temper boiled over. I went to push him away to get some distance between us, but something happened that I didn’t understand. Instead of pushing him I sent him flying back 15 feet, and right into a Passing Jedi on his way to one of the external landing pads.

    I turned to run but the Jedi found me, started speaking about how anger is a path to the darkside which I never understood. I’ve spent the last 3 years on Coruscant, the birth place of the Jedi order… Hopefully it will not be long before i get a master and hopefully I don’t get a stogy old man that prefers use of the force over a lightsaber. All the Mystic stuff might just make my head explode. Well that’s all for this entry, maybe I’ll do another one soon when I find out who my master is. Severice Ty’Laun signing off.

    Recording Three: Well, new update I guess. I’m no longer a Jedi, whoever see’s this might wonder why. The fact is simple, The council saw it a good idea to send two padawan’s…well one padawan, and me as an initiate. Stupid call if you ask me, I was honored at the time…but they didn’t prepare us for a confrontation with the darkside. The mission seemed simple, land at the ship yards on Abhean. Get information and leave. But it didn't end up that way, the padawan I was with didn’t last long, went all gung ho trying to take out a shuttle that was firing on us…sure I asked him if he could take care of it. But I expected him to be smart about it, but he wasn’t. The resistance seemed small at the time, all I saw was a single sith…or Sith spawn. But to make this short I’ll spare you all the long details.

    I lost, and I lost a leg…I expected to never wake up…but when I did I was in a cell on Korriban the birth place of the sith order…But what I never expected, was to wake up to see The high assassin himself. I was expecting an execution but that never happened. Instead he started training me and I soon learned that I was being held back by the Jedi. Here I was more free then I ever was as a jedi. Learning to overcome the disability of the lost leg, for weeks I endured harsh training but I never gave up…I gave into my anger, hate, and passion to improve. I am now a child of the dark side, a warrior in the making. This is the end of this recording. Who I once was, is dead.

    I have been reborn as Drakus and I’m signing out.
    _________________________________________

    Recording Four: This is Drakus once again, i've done it. What those Jedi held me back from doing, with the teachings that my master had taught me. I am no longer an Acolyte, i am now a full sith. My sabers have been unleashed and my chains have broken. Now ready to take on the Jedi, but i will choose my battles wisely. For i am not strong enough to go against the Jedi council just yet. For now i will train both myself and others. I have taken on two apprentices, one a female sith pureblood. Driven by the same thing i was when i started out in the academy, revenge. But now, i've seen the foolishness of that notion. I've come to realize, that we will never truely be rid of the Jedi as they will never be rid of us.

    It is a constant battle that we thrive from, yes in the past we have been defeated. But we have risen again, stronger then before. Taking the galaxy by surprise, because they were foolish and arrogant enough to think the sith could truly be destroyed. The second, a Chiss acolyte not really sure about this one...but soon i will see his true self. You never truly know someone till you cross blades with them. I guess this is a little update, and i will continue to record these...i guess this is my Legacy. Drakus signing off.

I guess my growth had caught the eye of someone up on high. My duties are going to be increasing and i guess i'll be used more in the war. I hope, i can't wait to show the Jedi what being left for dead will do to a man. I'm itching even more to get my hands on them, and one day..like the man that trained me...Drakus will be a name used to inspire fear, let this show you...if you have the resolve and will to work hard everyday...it doesn't take a lifetime to get some where. I'm 24 and i'm already a master, but then again...i always was a fast learner, and a hard worker. Now it's time to take another apprentice...or two. This is Sith Master Drakus Signing off....Hmm, i like the sound of that! End recording.

    Recording Six
    Well, i guess it's safe to say the major fighting of the war with the Alliance is done. Coruscant has fallen and lays in ruins, it was an amazing thing to feel the power the Empress and Dark lord command. I remember being on the observation spire of a Star destroyer with those that fought beside me at the Jedi temples main gate. The sensation given off though the force left me in Awe, though a very small part of me was saddened to see my home world in the condition it was left in. I had Left a Jedi, and returned a conqueror. A man betrayed, and his revenge fulfilled for the most part. That was immense power, when the ritual was done and i was in orbit i felt the force cry out. It was a victorious day. But the Jedi were far from wiped out, just scattered and labeled cowards and traitors. History was replaying itself before my vary eyes. The second Galactic Empire had taken ultimate power, now it was just a matter of time before the fleets turned their sights on the rest of the core and the planets that still resisted the Empress's rule. I will keep this holo journal updated after each Campaign comes to an end. End recording.

    Recording Seven
    I was correct, the fighting still continues. The Imperial fleets are engaged all over the core, chasing out the last resistance to us taking control of the core. My battle ground was Onderon, the fighting was long. A smaller force than what we employed on Coruscant. A smaller force, with never ending reinforcements switching out. As one of only two Sith Juggernauts in the order, i hold it as my duty to fight for the glory of the Empire. Having a Sith Master on the battlefield raised moral, while the rank and file got switched out. I refused to do the same, but i didn't come out unscathed. I received many blaster wounds, but nothing Bacta couldn't heal and i was back out in the fighting. Numerous soldiers fell to my lightsabers and i showed the superiority of my hybrid fighting style. With reports from the Wookie homeworld both Onderon, and Kashyyk have fallen. But now we have fallen still, our expansion stopped we are going to consolidate our power in the places we already control. I'll update this journal when something worth Mentioning happens. End this recording.

    Recording Eight
    Another update, it's been sometime since I've seen the Sith Temples. I continue to fight along side the Imperial Military, i've come to respect the men and women that make up our armed forces. Unlike me, they do not have the ability to use to force but they make due. For that, they earn my respect as I've earned there's. They know that as a Sith, i can do more and i don't need as much cover as they do. So during fighting, i can cover them to an extent by deflecting blaster fire while they flank. Well enough of that, Duros has fallen. I was part of what will be to be known as the Duros Genocide. But that is not the reason why i made this recording, i have taken lightsabers, Jedi lightsabers. From estimation some 40 Jedi had come to the Duros capital and died. A few by my blade, and because of my part in the war. I believe that i’ve caught the attention of the dark lord. Because i was one of the key leaders .

    On the other hand the long and bloody battle, on Corellia isen't going as smooth. Rumors are circulating that i'm up for rank advancement, Already? If these rumors are true, I'll be a Sith Lord, and one step closer to my goal of being on the Dark Council. I'm an ambitious Sith, true. This is Drakus, and i'm signing out till next time. I'm currently on route to Corellia with the next wave of reinforcements, I'll update this after the battle is done. I maybe over doing it, all the battles are taking it's toll, after this i'll take a break and recuperate. End this recording.

    Recording Nine
    As i thought, Corellia was a long battle. Those resistance fighters, they just didn't give up. Yet again, i've given respect to an enemy and i didn't hold back. My mastery of telekinesis has grown to an unprecedented level, my force repulse is a thing of utter destruction. I've shown that if given enough power to it, i can leave my enemies that are too close a pile of Ash. And the rumors were true, after the battle of Corellia the Empress have given me the rank of Sith Lord. And for my faithful service in the war so far. A seat on the dark council. I am the New Warmaster, and i take for myself the name of Darth Vyrassu.

    Ancient Sith for Domination, Thank you Sato for the Help with Ancient Sith. I will now take over the duty of leading the Imperial Military, and sit on the war council with Darth Vereor, the Dark lord of the Sith and Konstantine Arkyadvich, The high Marshal of the imperial military. The war is now in our hands, and nothing will stop us. This is Darth Vyrassu, End this recording.

    Recording Ten
    Okay, it's been awhile since I've recorded an entry. We've had to play the waiting game, our military council had agree'd to let the Chiss agents we had in play in the Hapan Consortium to do all the work. Manipulate events and strike when the time is right, and it worked. With their ruling houses and the upstart houses fighting each other we offered 'aid'. But in the end, we took over and seeing that we where the superior power. They fell in line, and the fall of the Hapan was complete. Now, it took was a bit longer then i would have liked to do this. Reports had said that the assault on Manaan had been a defeat. Now they are taking the stance they did back during Revan's time, it will only be a matter of time before the Hutts will have both us and the rebels by the short hairs. Bacta is running short, and Kolto isn't nearly as effective. It may even come to a point were both sides end up running out. Maybe in time we can make our own healing agent. Something for R&D to look into, and something i'll bring up to the Dark council when i get the chance. Now i have to go and make sure that this Sith Empire doesn't fall apart and become a thing of the past like many times before. Long live the Empire, and our Empress. This is Darth Vyrassu signing out, end recording.
