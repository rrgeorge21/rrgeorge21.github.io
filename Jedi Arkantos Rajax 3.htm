    Attributes 
    STRENGTH: Since the Fall of Coruscant Arkantos has taken to his physical training much more seriously, and focusing much more on the physical side of things, as to stay alive in this new Galaxy, where few Jedi survive.
    DEXTERITY: Keeping with his Mandalorian Special Ops training given to him since a young age, he's been able to stay quite agile and quick.

    Personality 

    Spending nearly a decade as a Bounty Hunter can change a man a bit, but Arkantos has stayed relatively the same throughout. He is a bit more serious, having to keep a more edged appearance in front of other criminals, and doesn't usually speak unless it's absolutely necessary, but this is usually in the criminal setting. He still longs to return to Tython and be surrounded by Nature once again, but realizes his mixing with the criminals is what has kept him alive all these years, avoiding the Empire's hunt for Jedi. He's done some things most Jedi would have frowned upon, such as assassinations, but has come to rationalize it by only taking jobs that involve hardened criminals, and living in a troubled time, one that the Jedi Order's teachings really hasn't been prepared for. He does the best he can to uphold his Jedi teachings, spending most of his off-time meditating and training his Lightsaber skills, though they have become much less frequent lately.

    While he still keeps his curiosity to learn and progress, he now sees that they must pertain pertain some aspect in his survival, especially while the Sith are hunting Jedi. His main goal is to re-establish a connection with the Jedi Order, though his hunt has gone dry, which gives him the idea that they're either all dead, or have become good at hiding, which is something of a double-edge sword. Still, he seeks to find his Master, Ashara Vashtee, above all.

    As far as philosophies go, Arkantos would lean more towards the Potentium view of the Force, though he has his spin on things. He theorizes that the Force isn't inherently good or evil, it does what it needs to survive (as all life does). He believes that the Force binds everything around them, and that all things and people are an extension of the Force and every Force user is a physical conduit for it. The evil and good aspect stems from each individual and their personal intentions, all depending on the user's emotions, the Force is then changed to reflect that person. It is not the Force that corrupts one's mind and power, but the user itself corrupting and manipulating the Force within himself, intensifying the original emotion. He believes the Force and oneself should be controlled and balanced, which is a main reason why he chose the Sentinel path within the Jedi, he seeks balance above all, with oneself and with everything around him.

    Biography 

    Arkantos Rajax was born to two Alliance citizens on Taris. His father was a retired soldier-turned-very successful merchant, and his mother was an accomplished military pilot. He has an older sibling named Sandy, two years his elder. She was very rambunctious, and it seemed to rub off on her little brother at an early stage of his life. While on a trade run to Roon, their ship crashed in a nearby meteor belt, after a failure with their targeting computers shortly before leaving hyperspace. The children managed to survive, being in the life support area of the freighter they were in, and luckily their mother maneuvered them from any substantial harm. Drifting through Roon space, a group of Mandalorian scouts managed to find them, thanks to the ship's emergency beacon, and brought the children to the planet. Arkantos was 5 at the time, and since then, the two children were taken under Clan Beviin's care.

    Raised by a single mother, a woman named Rheaia Beevin. Rheaia was a bit of a headstrong and vulgar woman, but very loving and protective of the two children. Rheaia was a Togorian commando, and lived in Sol'yc Arpat, a farming village on Roon. Most of Arkantos' childhood was constant teasing for Arkantos' curiosity and love for nature. Many in the village thought the boy too soft, and Rheaia usually had a blaster aimed at anyone who said that in her face. At a young age, Arkantos enjoyed to read, though his selection was limited to books of warfare and the Mandalorian culture. He didn't really enjoy his tenure as a Mandalorian, but he respected the culture that took him in, and cared for him. He was grateful, but he just felt like he didn't belong. He kept his mouth shut for most of his childhood as he trained to be one of Clan Beviin's future soldiers, but knew in his gut that there was more to the galaxy than just fighting for honor. He had a purpose in the world, other than just being a human in a suit of armor. He needed ideals that the Mandalorians didn't have. After finishing his Verd'goten, he became an adult and recruit for the Clan's special forces training. During his preliminary training, he began to have strange visions when dreaming. His mother thought that at first that they might just be dreams, but when the visions turned to reality, she then accepted that he might be sensitive to the Force. She told him, and cringed at the idea that he could use the Force like the Jedi or Sith, who were the culture's greatest rivals in the Galaxy.

    He began his research on the Jedi, and their way of thinking, and was ensnared by it, but knew that he'd dishonor his adoptive mother if he ever left. That didn't stop him from at least studying on them, remembering his original parent's stories about the Jedi. Rheaia grew furious when Sandy told her that Arkantos had started to look into the Jedi Order, enough to storm into his room, and began a fight with him. In a very heated disagreement, Arkantos left his home on Sol'yc Arpat and took a shuttle to Corellia. He spent months in communications with the Jedi Order and set up to be tested to become Initiated to the Order. He spend the next two years training at the Temple on Coruscant, soaking up all the information he could while there, awaiting his time until he is chosen to become a Padawan.

    After some years, he was picked up as Master Ashara Vashee's Padawan apprentice. He was talented in his own right, though Master Vashee was able to sculpt and train him to be whole, balanced in his own free-spirit nature by countering it with a classical and traditional Jedi Master who's focus was more on the nature of the Force than the blade like he was. He quickly rose to the title of a fully trained Jedi Knight. Through most of his Knighthood, he did mundane tasks around the Order, though kept to his training, but eventually ventured out to travel to visit other Force using groups. He eventually asked for a Padawan and was actually passed on one, a Mon Calamari whom he was aquanted with before, whom's previous Master reassigned after a prolonged mission. Arkantos took the Mon Calamari, who's name was Deenar Ica, with him in his travels, and eventually trained him up until the promotion of his Padawan into a Knight aswell. Shortly after, the War intensified, and Arkantos went on to fight in the front lines throughout many of the campaigns.

    He managed to narrowly escape the destruction and Fall of Coruscant, making his way to a Casino on the Wheel, eventually making his way back to his home planet on Roon, where he hoped he could retrieve his old set of Mandalorian Armor, in hopes of using it to easily move across the Galaxy, rather than a cloaked Force user. He was denied the armor of his Mandalorian youth, though he and his sister managed to infiltrate a research facility of a company testing a prototype armor. He stole it, and destroyed a large portion of the small space facility, and made his way back to Taris, his birth world. From there, he worked to erase his identity from any records indicating his life as a Jedi, which proved to be easier than he thought as he helped a local, well-known hacker with a few problems he had. Eventually, he went on to use his new identity to become a Bounty Hunter in Taris, and eventually grew in notoriety, and traveled to Hutt Space to find work. Finding his way into the Cartel, through Bounties, and through other tasks, he managed to get on the Cartel's good graces, and has been working for them since. He usually takes up very tough Infiltration and Assassinations, but isn't shy away from simple Mercenary guard work. He has even been known to take Jedi Bounties, something he has a record of never failing, though he has been using his front as a Bounty Hunter to get closer to other fugitive Jedi, hoping to help them fake their deaths, and get them somewhere safer, his ultimate goal: reconnect with the Jedi Order.

He seeks to reconnect with his old Padawan.
    • Ashara Vashee - Mother-figure. Arkantos' Master who he holds in the highest regards within the Jedi Order. He gravitated quickly to her during their short time as student and teacher, and see's her in a motherly light. He respects her, and her knowledge of the Jedi Order, and was enough to keep the young man in line. Would die for her. Currently his top Jedi priority is finding his Master's whereabouts since the end of the War.
    • Shi-Gel-Hu - Acquaintance. Met after the nautolan awoke from his comma. Aided him in cleaning his room. Rather enjoyed him, and considers him a good acquaintance, possibly a future friend.
