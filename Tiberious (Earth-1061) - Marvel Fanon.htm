Powers-[]

Immortality: As a Centivar, Tiberius has been granted immortality.

Abilities-[]

Super-Genius Intellect: His mind works at incredible speeds and is his greatest asset. His brain capacity has been increased to 90 percent. Able to think and perceive 9 times faster and utilize that much more of his mind than average human for information processing and sorting. He is capable of perfectly memorizing anything as well as being able to perfectly remember and instantly recall everything he saw and experienced in his lifetime without being overwhelmed or a pause for thought. Ares's mind is virtually a supercomputer built for strategy and problem-solving, one that works at optimal ability even when under stress and fatigue. He can work out a battle ahead of time for every possibility and predict enemy movements and tactics after the battle has engaged by instantly recalling and utilizing memorized mannerisms acquired through past experience. He is ingenious in; devising solutions against superior aspects of opponents, observing and exploiting, and can instantly make any calculations especially regarding distance, speed, and time; his sense of timing is perfect. He immediately solves any problem he happens to face and instantly formulates how to overcome any obstacle. He can understand the mechanics behind any issue he faces and knows what must be done to solve any problem.

Computer and Vehicles Expert: Ares is trained in the use of several computer systems and vehicle repairs as well as being an accomplished pilot.

Weapons Master: Master of numerous weapons, specifically a master marksman with skills in pistols, assault rifles, and sniper rifles. He is even a master swordsman, often using the Arc Blades of his Liveframe to great success. Ares learnt numerous martial arts skills from the system and can easily switch through combat styles as needed.

Master Assassin: Ares was both a highly skilled Assassin and a master warrior. He learned and developed a vast array of skills, such as picking pockets, blending into crowds, armed and unarmed combat, alternative or multiple weapon attacks, stealth and public assassination techniques, free-running and long-range combat.

Advanced Military Operator: Through access to the Sol Defense Network Database, Ares was well-versed in all armed force disciplines including intelligence gathering, escape arts, assassination, demolition, survival tactics, hunting, swimming, mountaineering, march or drill skills, map making, map reading, decoding cipher and other secret code messages, reading and making wood craft signs and other secret code languages, disguising, interrogation, computers, explosives, communication systems, vehicles, and electronic appliances used in armed forces.

Paraphernalia-[]

Cybernetic Spinal Implant: Ares's spine has been grafted with a cybernetic implant made from "Mysterium". This spinal implant enhances his physical abilities far past humanly possible via tapping into more of his brain capacity and channeling Phase Dimensional Energy through his body.

  • Physical Augmentation:
    • Supernatural Strength: Ares's strength has been augmented beyond the natural limits of the human race, his strength is superior to even the like of Cyborgs. He is strong enough to easily send a human flying a very far distance with a punch or a kick, out strength other superhuman opponents, and can lift roughly 8-10 tons at max.
    • Supernatural Stamina/Endurance: Ares's body is highly resistant to the build-up of fatigue toxins in his blood, allowing him to be physically active for considerably longer periods of time than the average member of the user's species. Additionally, he exert himself at peak capacity for hours, if not days before he gets exhausted.
    • Supernatural Speed: Ares's speed is similarly enhanced . He is fast enough to be a blur in front of a normal human's eyes, out speed or even outright blitz other superhumans, keep up and even surpass Lilith's combat and running speed. Finally, he can run at 85 mph and swim at 35 Knots at his peak.
    • Supernatural Reflexes: Ares has drastically enhanced reaction speed, allowing him to dodge bullets, catch flies in mid-air, dodge and maneuver around complex attacks, catch falling objects, block detriments and react instantaneously to what others take more time to react to.
    • Supernatural Agility: His agility has also augmented, he can go from one motion to another effortlessly, effectively dodge attacks, swing from things easily, sprint, do back-flips and numerous other gymnastic, athletic and martial implements with little effort.
  • Unlocked Mind:
    • Cosmic Awareness: This ability allowed him to know exactly what he needed to know at any moment. This could range from what his current location was to discovering an opponent's weakness
    • Hypercognition (Six Eyes): The Six Eyes provides the user with vast perception and immense brain processing power, allowing them to precisely manipulate the sophisticated energy powers down to an atomic level at a very efficient level. The mental prowess of the Six Eyes can turn one moment in reality into well over a minute's worth of time within the user's brain. This works well in tandem with the users enhanced sight, allowing them to analyze and calculate the positions of everything within view instantly. It allows the user to percieve and anlayze the precise flows of energy and movement to the point of even precognition.
    • Regeneration: He can heal/mend damaged or destroyed bodily tissue with far greater speed and efficiency than an ordinary human. Able to recover from virtually any non-lethal wound including injured tissues and missing organs. Gunshot and stab wounds, cuts, and broken bones can perfectly heal in several hours.
  • Mysterium Properties:
    • Conductivity: Mysterium conducts electricity at 100% efficiency. However, unlike other metals, mysterium barely conducts or retains heat. Additionally, mysterium is radiation-proof.
    • Virtual Invincibility: Stronger than even adamantium and is virtually indestructiable. It can even absorb energy to slowly repair itself if not completely destroyed.
    • Anti-Magic: It is revealed that mysterium can also negate the effects of magic. Even touching it can break spells and wards.

Phase Dimension Connection: As a side effect of his connection to the Phase Dimension, he can draw upon its energy for various purposes.

  • Phase Ergokinesis: Manipulation of the Phase energy on various levels.
    • Energy Channeling: Ares can channel the raw energies of the Phase Dimension into the material world often to increase the effectiveness of items and technology.
    • Simple Energy Contructs: He is able to often create simple and basic objects like staffs, blades, barriers, orbs, etc.
  • Phasestep: High Speed Movement that appears almost like teleportation in that he can "phase" through walls/objects (amplified by his Phasemass skill).
  • Phasemass: A highly versatile ability with multiple sub-abilties that involve Mass Effect Fields. His manipulation of these fields are so smooth and tempered that it almost seemed simple. He is able to feel the fields of mass and gravity vector, permeating the surrounding space. It operates not with separate vectors and parts of space, but with whole planes and "gravitational currents" in them. It operates much like telekinesis on a instinctual and reflexive level. He is also able to create shields, blasts, enhancements, and even warp across the battlefield. Ares often combines his powers together with his acrobatics, CQC, and marksman skills. Thanks to a his Mysterium augment, his power levels can even be augmented by ambient heat, magnetism or existing dark energy.
