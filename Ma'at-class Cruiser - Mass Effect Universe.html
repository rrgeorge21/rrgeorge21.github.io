        Information
        The Amun Sovereignty and the Amun Starship Technologies (AST) corporation had a co-dependency for decades. AST designed, built, and maintained the ships from the Amun Sovereignty Defense Force (ASDF) since its inception. In fact, all of the ships in the Amun Sovereignty Navy come from AST. The Ma'at-class cruiser is the workhorse of the ASDF. Unfortunately, at the time, there was not enough credits to buy many of these ships. As such, only 15 were left in service once AST went out of business and the Reaper War ended. However, the Amun Sovereignty wished to continue to produce the Ma'at-class cruiser. As such, the ASDF bought the rights to the cruiser from the controlling entities of AST. With the Amun Sovereignty having their own shipyard almost completed, they fully intend on producing more of these ships as their main line cruiser.

        The Ma'at-class cruiser was designed to balance size and speed. It possesses a commendable complement of ordinance and performs well in ship maneuvers. However, there are some disadvantages to the design. All of the torpedo tubes are forward-facing, so it packs a punch head on and lacks punch to the rear. In addition, its maneuverability has a cost--it burns a lot of fuel with its 10 maneuvering thrusters. ASDF engineers are trying to replicate the maneuvering thrusters of the Citadel Alliance ships, but still have a long way to go in that area.


        Availability for Purchase
        - Amun Sovereignty: AVAILABLE
        - All Others: UNAVAILABLE
