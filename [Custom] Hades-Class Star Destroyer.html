Hades-Class Star Destroyer



Ship Class: Light Star Destroyer
Ship Role: Star Destroyer/Command Ship
Ship Length: 1,020 meters
Agility (A): 2
Defensive (D): 4
Offensive (O): 5
Speed (S): 3
Special Gear: 3 (Hangar Space, Troop Capacity, Advanced Jammer Suite, Backup Shield Generators)

Weaponry:
Electromagnetic Plasma Cannons: 2
Heavy turbolaser batteries: 40
Dual turbolaser batteries: 60
Heavy ion cannons: 12
Standard ion cannons: 20
Proton torpedo launchers (40 rounds per launcher): 12
Point defense cannons: 52
Phylon Q7 tractor beam projectors: 10

Description: Designed by The Organization and built by Aegohr Manufacturing, deep within the asteroid field that also house the fabled Smuggler's Run, the Hades-Class Star Destroyer was one of the most ambitious undertakings done by The Organization to date. Modifying their design and constructing the ship around two Electromagnetic Plasma Cannons, stacked on top of each other, the ship is otherwise bristling with weaponry. Boasting 40 heavy turbolasers batteries, 60 dual turbolasers batteries, 12 heavy ion cannons, 20 ion cannons, 12 proton torpedo launchers, 52 point defence lasers and 10 tractor beam projectors, the Hades was an implement of war and destruction, designed specifically to do one job; crush the enemies of The Organization mercilessly. Carrying 48 starfighters, 2 Light Freighters, and around 8-16 utility craft for support combined with a complement of 1,500 soldiers for ground deployments, the Hades was an impressive addition to the Organization's Navy.
